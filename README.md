# Sales-and-Performance-Analysis
Sales &amp; Performance Analysis project using Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Plotly to analyze regional sales, profit trends, and category performance with interactive visualizations.

I developed a Sales & Performance Analysis project as part of my Data Science learning to understand how data can be used to support business decision-making. The primary objective of this project was to analyze company sales data and generate meaningful insights related to sales performance, profit trends, regional growth, and product category performance.

In this project, I used Python along with important data science libraries such as Pandas and NumPy for data manipulation and analysis. For data visualization, I used Matplotlib and Plotly to create both basic and interactive charts. I created a structured dataset that included fields like Order ID, Region, Category, Sales, Profit, and Quantity to simulate real-world business data.

I began by exploring the dataset to understand its structure, checking for missing values, and reviewing summary statistics. After preparing the data, I performed analysis to answer key business questions. These included calculating total sales by region, identifying the most profitable product categories, and finding the top 5 highest sales orders.

Additionally, I introduced a new metric called Profit Ratio, calculated as Profit divided by Sales, to measure business efficiency. This helped in comparing performance across different regions and identifying areas where profitability could be improved.

To present the findings effectively, I created several visualizations. A bar chart was used to display sales by region, a pie chart showed profit distribution by category, a scatter plot helped analyze the relationship between sales and profit, and a box plot was used to study profit ratio distribution across regions.

Overall, this project enhanced my skills in data analysis, visualization, and problem-solving. It also provided hands-on experience in working with business data and applying data science concepts to real-world scenarios, helping me build a strong foundation in data analytics.
